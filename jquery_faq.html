Europa

  • Orbits Jupiter
  • Named after Europa (consort of Zeus), a Phoenician princess in Greek mythology, from whom the name of the continent Europe is taken
  • Also one of the Oceanids, the daughters of the Titan Oceanus
  • Slightly smaller than Earth's moon

Hyperion

  • Orbits Saturn
  • The first non-round moon to be discovered
  • Also a great series of sci-fi novels by Dan Simmons
  • One of the twelve Titan children of Gaia (Earth) and Uranus (Sky) who, led by Cronus, overthrew their father Uranus and were themselves later overthrown by the Olympians.

Io

  • Orbits Jupiter
  • Can stand for Input/Ouput
  • In mythology, a daughter of Inachus in Greek mythology, and lover of Zeus who was turned into a cow
